Data Analysis Project - Sales Data Analysis MeriSKILL
Purpose: To analyze the Sales data and extract meaningful insights out of it for making key Business decisions

Step:1 Overall Analysis...
Basic Stats - Total Orders, Total Quantity ordered, Total Sales, Total Products, Average Order value. Then dive into insights like...
Total Sales by Hour
Total Sales by City
Total Sales by Month
Total Customers by Month

Step:2 Product wise...
Basic Stats - Total Orders, Product Categories. Then...
Top 5 Products by Price
Top 5 Products by Quantity
Total Products by Day
Total Products by Month
Products sold on Weekday/Weekend

Step3: City wise...
Basic Stats - Total Orders, Average Orders per City Then...
Total Orders by City
Total Sales by City
Weekend/Weekday Sales by City

Finally a filter to view the insights Quarterly.
